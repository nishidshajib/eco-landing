Credit Score Impact on Texas Rates

Texas lenders price in tiers: 780+, 760–779, 740–759, 720–739, 700–719, 680–699, 660–679, 640–659. Each tier shifts APR by 0.125%–0.50%.

Rate Tier Pricing

740+ gets best rates; 680–719 pays 0.50%–1.00% more; below 640 faces steep pricing.

Monthly Payment Impact

On a $300K loan, moving from 680 to 740 saves $50–100/month.

Optimization Strategy

Rapid rescores, utilization drops, and dispute fixes can jump you into a better tier before locking.
