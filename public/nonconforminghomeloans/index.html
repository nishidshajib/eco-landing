Jumbo Loans (Above Conforming Limits)

High-balance mortgages exceeding $766,550 conforming limit (2024) for luxury properties and expensive markets. Jumbo loans require 700+ credit, 10-20% down payment, 43% max DTI, and 6-12 months reserves. Rates typically 0.25-0.75% higher than conforming loans, but flexible underwriting available through jumbo specialists.

Bank Statement Loans (Self-Employed)

Non-QM loans using 12-24 months business or personal bank statements instead of tax returns for self-employed borrowers, business owners, and 1099 contractors. Verify income through deposits rather than W-2s—ideal for borrowers with complex deductions who show lower taxable income. Requires 660+ credit and 10-20% down through specialized non-QM lenders.

Asset-Based Mortgages (High Net Worth)

Qualify based on liquid assets (stocks, bonds, retirement accounts, real estate equity) rather than employment income. Lenders calculate monthly "asset income" by dividing portfolio value by 60-120 months. Perfect for retirees, executives with stock compensation, or high-net-worth individuals with fluctuating income. Understanding Conforming vs. Non-Conforming Loan Limits

Conforming loans capped at $766,550 in most areas (2024), higher in expensive counties like San Francisco ($1,149,825) and NYC ($970,800). Non-conforming (jumbo) loans start where conforming limits end—financing luxury properties, high-cost markets, and multi-million dollar homes. Understanding which loan type you need based on purchase price affects rate, down payment, and documentation through Browse Lenders.

Jumbo Loan Credit Score Requirements: 700-740+ Tiers

Jumbo lenders require higher credit standards: 700-719 qualifies but pays premium rates, 720-739 gets standard jumbo pricing, 740+ accesses best rates (within 0.125-0.25% of conforming). Every 20-point improvement saves 0.125-0.25% on rate—$30-$60/month on $1.5M loan. Check your middle credit score before applying to understand pricing tier.

Down Payment Strategies for Jumbo Mortgages

Jumbo loans require 10-20% down depending on loan amount: $766,551-$1.5M requires 10-15% down, $1.5M-$2.5M requires 15-20% down, $2.5M+ requires 20-30% down. Some portfolio lenders accept 10% down with PMI on jumbos up to $2M. Consider cash-out refinance on current home for jumbo down payment.

Bank Statement Loans: Qualifying Without Tax Returns

Self-employed borrowers, business owners, and 1099 contractors can qualify using 12-24 months bank statements instead of tax returns. Lenders average monthly deposits and apply 50% expense ratio to calculate qualifying income. Example: $30,000/month deposits × 50% = $15,000 qualifying income. Rates 0.50-1.25% higher than conventional loans but accessible through specialized non-QM lenders.
