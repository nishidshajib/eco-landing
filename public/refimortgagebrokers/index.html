Refinance Rate Shopping Essentials

What you need to know before comparing lender rates through brokers

How Broker Rate Shopping Works

Mortgage brokers submit your scenario to multiple wholesale lenders simultaneously, comparing rates, fees, and terms across the market. This wholesale access often beats retail bank pricing by 0.125-0.375% in rate or thousands in fees—brokers get paid by lenders, not you.

Timing Your Rate Lock Strategy

Brokers help you decide when to lock your rate based on market trends and closing timeline. Lock too early and you miss rate drops; lock too late and rates might spike. Broker market expertise guides this critical timing decision that can save or cost thousands.

Credit Score Impact on Broker Quotes

Your middle credit score determines which lenders offer best pricing through brokers. Scores above 740 unlock top-tier rates; scores below 680 limit lender options but brokers know which ones are most forgiving. Small credit improvements before applying create significant rate reductions.

Rate vs. Fees Trade-Offs

Lower rates often come with higher fees through discount points. Brokers calculate which combination minimizes total cost based on your break-even timeline. Sometimes a slightly higher rate with lower fees saves more money if you sell or refinance again within 5-7 years.

How Credit Scores Affect Broker Rate Shopping Results

Your middle credit score determines which wholesale lenders offer best pricing through brokers and how aggressively brokers can negotiate on your behalf. Higher scores unlock more lender options and better rates—knowing where you stand helps brokers shop strategically.

620-679: Broker-Dependent Refinancing

Middle credit scores limit direct lender options but brokers know which wholesale lenders specialize in this range. Expect higher rates than top-tier but brokers can still find competitive pricing through lender relationships. Strategic credit improvement before applying helps brokers secure better terms.

680-739: Competitive Broker Rates

Good credit opens up most wholesale lenders and allows brokers to negotiate effectively. Rates improve significantly above 700 and brokers can leverage multiple lender quotes to drive pricing down. This range offers strong value through broker rate shopping versus going direct.

740+: Premium Broker Pricing

Excellent credit qualifies for top-tier wholesale rates and maximum broker negotiating power. Brokers can secure the absolute lowest market rates through aggressive lender competition. Small rate differences at this tier create major lifetime savings—broker expertise matters most here.
