Questions Florida Jumbo Buyers Ask

Get clarity on high-balance loan requirements and Florida-specific challenges.

Conforming loan limits vary by county. Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach have higher limits than inland counties. Any loan above the county limit requires jumbo financing with stricter guidelines.

Jumbo loans carry more risk because they exceed government-backed limits. Lenders compensate by requiring 700+ scores, lower debt-to-income ratios, and larger cash reserves.

Waterfront properties require wind and flood insurance that complicate underwriting. Lenders want proof of coverage and reserve funds to cover higher premiums before approving jumbo loans.

Non-warrantable condos—those with litigation, rental ratios above limits, or incomplete projects—require portfolio jumbo lenders willing to accept higher risk in exchange for stricter terms.
