Credit Score Impact on California Jumbo Rates

Jumbo lenders price in tight credit tiers. Moving from 720 to 740 can save 0.25%–0.50% APR—worth $300–600/month on a $2M California loan.

Jumbo Credit Tiers

760+ gets best jumbo pricing; 740–759 adds 0.125%–0.25%; 720–739 adds 0.375%–0.625%; below 700 faces steep pricing or denial.

Monthly Payment Impact

On a $2M jumbo, moving from 725 to 750 credit saves $300–500/month.

Reserve & Credit Synergy

High reserves (12+ months PITI) can offset lower credit (700–720). Portfolio lenders weight compensating factors.

California Jumbo Questions

Common questions about jumbo loans and portfolio lenders in California.

$1,149,825 for most California counties (2025 high-balance conforming limit). Loans above this require jumbo financing. Some rural counties have lower limits ($766,550).

Most jumbo lenders require 10%–20% down. Some portfolio lenders offer 10% down jumbo with strong credit (740+) and reserves. Super-jumbo ($2M+) often requires 20%–25% down.

Minimum 700 for most jumbo lenders; best pricing starts at 740+. Portfolio lenders may approve 680–700 with compensating factors (high reserves, low LTV, strong income).

6–12 months of PITI (principal, interest, taxes, insurance) in liquid reserves. California property taxes are high (1%+ of value), so PITI is higher than other states. More reserves help approval.
