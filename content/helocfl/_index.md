A **home equity line of credit (HELOC)** is a revolving credit line secured by your home's equity. HELOCs offer flexibility to borrow and repay during the draw period with variable rates that adjust with market conditions.

Through <a href="https://www.browselenders.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">our lender directory</a>, <a href="https://www.middlecreditscore.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">credit score education</a>, and <a href="https://www.homeloancalculator.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">home equity calculators</a>, Florida homeowners build HELOC literacy and learn how to use home equity strategically without overborrowing.
