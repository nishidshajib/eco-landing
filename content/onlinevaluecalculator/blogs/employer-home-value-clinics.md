Forward-thinking employers are weaving housing literacy into their 2026 wellness stacks. By underwriting a quarterly Home Value Clinic, companies help team members manage relocations, equity taps, and renovation financing without the stress that usually tanks productivity. Online Value Calculator is rolling out a turnkey curriculum that HR leaders can deploy with minimal lift.
