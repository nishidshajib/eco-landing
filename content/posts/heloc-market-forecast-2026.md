As we look ahead to 2026, understanding HELOC market trends and rate forecasts can help borrowers make informed decisions about timing their home equity access. Here's what industry experts are predicting.

## Federal Reserve Policy Outlook

### Current Economic Environment (Late 2025):
- Federal funds rate: 5.25-5.50%
- Inflation trending toward 2% target
- Labor market showing signs of cooling
- Economic growth moderating but positive

### 2026 Rate Predictions:
**Consensus Forecast:**
- 2-3 rate cuts likely in first half of 2026
- Federal funds rate ending 2026: 4.50-5.00%
- Prime rate forecast: 7.50-8.00%
- HELOC rates: 8.00-9.50% (prime + margin)

### Factors Influencing Rate Direction:

#### Supportive for Rate Cuts:
- Continued inflation decline
- Labor market normalization
- Economic growth moderation
- Improved supply chain conditions

#### Risk Factors for Higher Rates:
- Persistent services inflation
- Geopolitical tensions
- Energy price volatility
- Fiscal policy changes
