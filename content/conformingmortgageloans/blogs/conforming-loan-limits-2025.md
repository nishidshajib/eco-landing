# 2025 Conforming Loan Limits: Essential Guide for Middle Credit Score Borrowers

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) has announced the 2025 conforming loan limits, bringing exciting opportunities for middle credit score borrowers seeking safe, government-backed financing through Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac programs.

## What Are Conforming Loan Limits?

Conforming loan limits represent the maximum amount you can borrow through Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac programs while maintaining government-sponsored enterprise (GSE) backing. These limits ensure loans remain within the "conforming" category, offering borrowers enhanced security and standardized terms.

## 2025 Conforming Loan Limits by Area

### Standard Areas
- **Single-family homes**: $766,550 (up from $740,000)
- **Two-unit properties**: $981,948
- **Three-unit properties**: $1,186,350  
- **Four-unit properties**: $1,474,400

### High-Cost Areas
- **Single-family homes**: Up to $1,149,825
- **Luxury markets**: Individual county determinations
- **California/New York metros**: Premium limit applications
