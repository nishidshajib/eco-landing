## Rate Lock Strategies for Middle Credit Borrowers

### Timing Rate Locks
**Market volatility considerations:**
- **Rate lock periods**: 30, 45, 60, 90 days typically available
- **Extension options**: Usually 0.125-0.250% per 15-30 day extension
- **Float-down options**: Some lenders offer rate decreases during lock period
- **Credit score timing**: Lock rates after score improvements are reflected

### Optimal Lock Timing
**For middle credit borrowers:**

**Lock immediately when:**
- **Rates are rising** rapidly in current market
- **Credit score** has been recently optimized  
- **Home shopping** timeline is compressed
- **Lender competition** has identified best rate

**Consider floating when:**
- **Rates are declining** or expected to fall
- **Credit score improvement** is in progress
- **Home shopping** timeline is extended  
- **Market volatility** suggests waiting
